About SmartMed

SmartMed is a Medicine Shop Automation (MSA) system designed for retail medicine shops that manage a large variety of medicines from multiple vendors. It helps shop owners maintain minimal inventory using a Just-in-Time inspired philosophy while ensuring continuous sales through automated reorder alerts. Key capabilities include threshold calculation (based on average weekly sales), daily expiry reports, vendor-wise expired item lists, cheque generation for vendors on supply, printing cash receipts for sales, and generating revenue/profit reports for any period.

Threshold & Reorder

System calculates per-item reorder thresholds using average weekly sales so owners can reorder just-in-time and avoid excess stock.

Expiry Management

Generate daily list of expired items and vendor-wise expired lists so replacements can be requested automatically.

Vendor Management

Store vendor name, address and codes; print cheques and track vendor-wise payments and supplied medicine codes.

Receiving Supply

Enter item code, batch number, expiry and quantity on receipt; system prints supply acknowledgement and updates stock.

Sales & Receipts

Enter code and quantity sold to generate cash receipt instantly; sales update inventory and feed into revenue reports.

Revenue & Profit

Generate period-wise revenue and profit statements and vendor-wise payment summaries for accounting and auditing.
